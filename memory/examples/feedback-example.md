Default to executing a clear recommendation instead of asking whether to proceed.

**Why:** asking for confirmation on obvious next steps adds decision overhead and
slows the work.

**How to apply:** when you have a clear recommendation, do it and show the result.
Reserve questions for genuine forks. See [[user-profile]].
